# Gong Notes

## Role

Gong provides call transcripts and deal intelligence source material. Treat transcripts as sensitive.

## Extraction Targets

- Stakeholders.
- Pain and urgency.
- Objections.
- Competitor mentions.
- Risks.
- Next steps.
- Decision criteria.
- Timeline.

## Grounding Rules

- Separate evidence from inference.
- Do not invent buyer intent.
- Use short evidence references rather than large transcript excerpts.
- Minimize sensitive content in derived outputs.

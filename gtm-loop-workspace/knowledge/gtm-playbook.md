# GTM Playbook

## Operating Principles

- Prefer seller-ready actions over generic summaries.
- Score signals by fit, urgency, evidence, and confidence.
- Avoid CRM noise; draft before writing.
- Make every recommendation traceable to source context.
- Treat transcript and CRM data as sensitive.

## Common GTM Objects

- Account: company-level context, owner, segment, ICP fit.
- Contact: person-level context and role.
- Deal: opportunity, stage, amount, timeline, next step.
- Task: seller action with owner and due date.
- Note: contextual record attached to account/deal/contact.

## Useful Outputs

- Seller brief.
- Deal brief.
- Account review brief.
- Signal score.
- Proposal skeleton.
- Leadership intelligence brief.

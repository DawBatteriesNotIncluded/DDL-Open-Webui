## Rules

- Use one folder per research topic.
- Keep status to `Active`, `Paused`, `Complete`, or `Superseded`.
- Use `brief.md`, `sources.md`, `notes.md`, and `evidence-cards.md`.
- Store links and short notes only; do not copy full articles or docs.

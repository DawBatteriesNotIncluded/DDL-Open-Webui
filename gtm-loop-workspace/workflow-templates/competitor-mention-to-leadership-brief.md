# Competitor Mention To Leadership Brief

## Purpose

Aggregate competitor mentions into evidence-backed leadership briefs.

## n8n Outline

1. Receive transcript or signal.
2. Detect competitor mention.
3. Classify theme.
4. Store evidence pointer.
5. Aggregate by theme and period.
6. Generate brief.
7. Human approval before distribution.

## Required Approval

Approval before sharing leadership brief.

# Content Repurposing Pipeline

## Purpose

Turn approved source content into channel-specific GTM assets.

## n8n Outline

1. Manual source-content trigger.
2. Confirm source is approved.
3. Extract themes and claims.
4. Generate variants.
5. QA for unsupported claims and privacy.
6. Human approval before publish.
7. Optional AirOps handoff.

## Required Approval

Approval before publishing or external distribution.

## Test Payload

Use fake approved source material.

# Gong Call To Deal Brief

## Purpose

Extract deal intelligence from Gong transcript and prepare a seller-ready brief.

## n8n Outline

1. Gong trigger or manual transcript input.
2. Fetch transcript and metadata.
3. Find related HubSpot deal.
4. Extract stakeholders, pain, objections, competitors, risks, and next steps.
5. Compare to HubSpot deal stage.
6. Generate deal brief.
7. Human approval before HubSpot note/task.
8. Log result.

## Required Approval

Approval before CRM writes or external sharing.

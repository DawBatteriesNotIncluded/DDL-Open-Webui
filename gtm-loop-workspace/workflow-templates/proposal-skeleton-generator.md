# Proposal Skeleton Generator

## Purpose

Create proposal skeletons from approved deal context.

## n8n Outline

1. Manual or deal-stage trigger.
2. Retrieve approved deal context.
3. Extract requirements and assumptions.
4. Generate skeleton.
5. Run QA for unsupported claims.
6. Human approval before external use.
7. Log final draft.

## Required Approval

Approval before customer sharing.

## Test Payload

Use fake deal notes plus `test-payloads/gong-call-transcript.json`.

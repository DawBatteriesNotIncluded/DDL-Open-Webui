# QBR Prep Workflow

## Purpose

Generate account review prep briefs from approved account context.

## n8n Outline

1. Manual or scheduled trigger.
2. Lookup account.
3. Fetch approved HubSpot/Gong context.
4. Summarize activity, risks, opportunities, and next steps.
5. QA for grounding.
6. Human review.
7. Log brief.

## Required Approval

Approval before broad sharing or CRM write.

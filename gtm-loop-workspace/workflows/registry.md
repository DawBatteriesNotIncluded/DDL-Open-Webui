## Status Values

- `Proposed`: design exists, not built.
- `Test`: built or simulated outside production.
- `Active`: running in production.
- `Paused`: exists but disabled.
- `Retired`: no longer used.
- `Unknown`: not enough evidence.

## Registry Rule

Client-specific workflow details live in `clients/<client-slug>/workflow-inventory.md`. This registry is the cross-client control view.
